Alex Standall

To be accepted into the popular group he paid a higher price than before, of being neglectful of his skinny vacation friend, Hannah. However, he never lost his cynicism about the successful boy script they were trying to implant in him. In a decisive moment in the series, he was the only one capable of highlighting all of his friends' rot, exposed the wounds of all those accused who tried to plot against Hannah, and referred to himself as a "pathetic and weak loser". His brutal coherence, misguided by the desire to belong, left him burdened with remorse and destructive actions. Not everyone notices, but he was already showing risky behavior in apparent accidents, by "falling" into the pool, driving at high speed, getting involved in a fight, in other words, he was showing signs of final and fatal suicide.
