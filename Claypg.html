Clay Jensen

17-year-old Clay Jensen returns home from school to find a mysterious box on his porch. Inside, he discovers seven double-sided cassette tapes recorded by Hannah Baker, his deceased colleague who committed suicide two weeks earlier. On each side of the tapes, Hannah develops an intensely emotional audio diary detailing why she decided to end her life. It seems that every person who receives this package of cassette tapes is one of the reasons why they killed themselves. After listening to the tapes, he must pass the tapes to someone else. There appears to be a distribution order for the tapes, and if anyone decides to break the chain, a copy of the tapes will be released to the public. Each tape recording refers to a different person involved in Hannah's life, contributing to a reason for her suicide mostly referring to her friends and enemies...
